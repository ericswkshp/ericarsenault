About me

I spent most of my adult life working in the skilled trades, from repairing helicopter structures in the Army to welding on nuclear submarines and aircraft carriers for the Navy. It was a privelege to experience those careers, but after becoming a dad, I decided to pursue something less hazardous. A friend suggested I learn to write some code. I was instantly hooked after only a few minutes. I didn't know what I was doing, but I loved it, so I decided to go back to college and pursue a degree in Website Programming. I'm absolutely stoked to get to work on these websites each day. Whether it's for a client or for school, I know I'm going to learn something new every single day.
